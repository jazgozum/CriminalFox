WELCOME TO THE Solana Elites Ape

The Solana Elites Ape is a limited collection of 1200 "Elites Apes” that reside on Solana Island in the heart of the Bermuda Triangle. Inspired by our fellow Bored Apes on the Ethereum chain, we are here to bring the Solana Elites Ape to the Solana blockchain. ...In a Island littered by the remnants of a past life, Elites Ape are locked in an eternal struggle to survive, which allows them to further their bloodline. While their incentives may not always align, the Elites Ape from all corners of the globe are faced with extremely difficult odds and recognize their best chance at survival often lies in cooperation.

Roadmap

Road to be a one of the Blue Chip on Solana

Fair Launch

Our first and number one priority is ensuring that the Solana Elites Ape project has a smooth and successful mint. We spent a ton of time creating the art and now we are in the process of building an amazing community that we hope not only loves the project now

Market Listing

Shortly after our mint is done, we will launch the Solana Elites Ape on secondary marketplaces. For now the goal will be to get them on Magic Eden and Solanart.

DAO Channel

Once you have your Elites Ape in your wallet you will be able to access the exclusive Ferry Club for verified owners. This is where most of the ongoing announcements and value for the community will be added..

Token Airdrops.

As part of our plan to give back to the community, we are going to Airdrop our own Token to every Elites Ape Holder and The token that we are going to airdrop is going to be swappable via USDT.

Community Vault

The community wallet will receive not only funds from the team, but also a percentage of secondary royalties. The 50% of royalties will be used as a liquidity pool of our token, we are imagining that it will fund giveaways, merchandise, and social event for verified Elites Ape holders.

DAO Merchandise

Having members on the team with a background in e-commerce, we plan to have physical merchandise available exclusively for verified Elites Ape holders. We will be involving the community a lot for this project to ensure that we are creating something that people want to represent..
