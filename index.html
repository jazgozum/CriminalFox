STORY

The Manji gang is formed by several prisoners after they escape from the jail. The members’ burning passion and rebellious attitude made the gang grow. Few months later, the Manji mistook someone for their target. So one of...the members worry that they might be innocent, but the other one still insist that whether it is the actual target or the mistaken one, they better make sure no one is getting in their way. The Manji’s notable victim turns out to be of a poor family, much the son’g agony. That son vowed revenge on the Manjis for the death of his family. He roamed on the streets until he found an undergound place where his soon-to-be-friends dwell. Coincidently, they also turn out to be victims of the Manjis, so they together form the Valhalla gang in hopes of seeking revenge on their rival gang.
