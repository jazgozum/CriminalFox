Roadmap

Road to be a one of the Blue Chip on Solana

Fair Launch

Our first and number one priority is ensuring that the Adult Ape Ferry Club project has a smooth and successful mint. We spent a ton of time creating the art and now we are in the process of building an amazing community that we hope not only loves the project now

Secondary Market Listing

Shortly after our mint is done, we will launch the Adult Ape Ferry Club on secondary marketplaces. For now the goal will be to get them on Magic Eden and Solanart.

Verified Holders Channel

Once you have your Adult Ape in your wallet you will be able to access the exclusive Ferry Club for verified owners. This is where most of the ongoing announcements and value for the community will be added..

Community Vault.

The community wallet will receive not only funds from the team, but also a percentage of secondary royalties. The 50% of royalties will be used as a liquidity pool of our token, we are imagining that it will fund giveaways, merchandise, and social event for verified Adult Ape holders.

Token Airdrops

As part of our plan to give back to the community, we are going to Airdrop our own Token to every Adult Ape Holder and The token that we are going to airdrop is going to be swappable via USDT.

Exclusive Merchandise

Having members on the team with a background in e-commerce, we plan to have physical merchandise available exclusively for verified Adult Ape holders. We will be involving the community a lot for this project to ensure that we are creating something that people want to represent..
